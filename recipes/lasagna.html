Lasagna Recipe

Description

Ingredients

  • Meat: sweet Italian sausage and lean ground beef
  • Lasagna noodles
  • 1 onion
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans tomato sauce
  • 2 cans tomato paste
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Mozzarella
  • Parmesan
  • 1 egg
  • Fresh parsley
  • Dried basil leaves
  • Italian seasoning
  • Fennel seeds
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Prepare meat sauce
  2. Cook noodles
  3. Make ricotta mixture
  4. Layer lasagna
  5. Cover with foil
  6. Bake
